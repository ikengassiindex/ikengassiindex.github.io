2

Normalise

Four normalisation methods: Method A (robust fleet percentile P5/P95), Method B (standard fleet percentile), Method C (bounded rescaling), and Method D (categorical mapping). Inverted metrics for density measures where higher = better resilience.

3

Weight

6-level hierarchical weighting across 6 components and 20 metrics. Continuity dominates at 0.30, followed by Infrastructure (0.25) and Saturation (0.20). Weight budget validated by Sobol sensitivity analysis.

4

Compose R_base

Weighted sum of 6 normalised component scores produces the base resilience score.

5

Modify

Six multiplicative modifiers adjust R_base for context: R2 (adaptive climate IRI), R3 (consequence + energy poverty), R4 (graph-theoretic network criticality), R6a (restoration speed), R6b (network topology), R7 (digital readiness proxy). Plus 5 micro-enrichments for vulnerable populations, flood zones, landslides, digital capabilities, and healthcare criticality.

6
