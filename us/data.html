SSI Engine

Client-side formula implementation

The SSI v4.0.2 calculation engine is implemented in ssi-engine.js and runs entirely in the browser. It exposes the complete formula construct: 6 component weights, 20 metric intra-weights, all 6 modifier functions (R2 adaptive IRI, R3 consequence sigmoid, R4 graph criticality, R6a restoration speed, R6b network topology, R7 digital readiness), T1 DER stress composite, normalisation methods, soft-clip overflow compression, and a client-side Monte Carlo simulation (1,000 iterations for browser performance).
