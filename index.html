The First Open Digital Twin Fusing Grid Risk with Societal Exposure

A composite resilience score for European power grids — open data, open methodology, zero proprietary dependencies. Select a country to explore its digital twin.

Operated as a non-profit through a dedicated foundation — ensuring the Index serves the public interest.
Breaking the Silos

Power grid resilience is inseparable from its socio-economic context. The SSI fuses traditional grid metrics with socio-economic, environmental, and energy-transition data — all from public sources — to capture what single-discipline indices miss.

Pan-European Coverage

Starting with Italy and Germany, expanding to Switzerland and Austria. Each country's digital twin uses the same SSI v4.0.2 methodology adapted to local data sources, enabling cross-border comparison and European-level risk insights.
